caprese

simple Italian dish, traditionally with fresh roma tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, fresh basil, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, and freshly cracked pepper. it can be simplified into tomato + cheese + balsamic.

this one sandy didn't actually make

ingredients

  • roma tomato
  • parmigiano reggiano
  • balsamic vinegar
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • black pepper

steps
  1. slice tomatoes 1 unit wide
  2. shave parmesan 0.5 units wide
  3. plate tomatoes across plate
  4. place parmesan on top of tomatoes
  5. drizzle 1 unit balsamic vinegar on top
  6. drizzle 0.75 unit olive oil on top
  7. crack any amount black pepper
